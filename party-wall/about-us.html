At Jason Edworthy, we understand that construction projects—whether a loft conversion, extension, or excavation—can be both exciting and stressful. Our mission is to ensure that all parties involved—whether building or adjoining owners—are protected, informed, and satisfied. We act as impartial experts, delivering solutions that save time, reduce conflicts, and keep your project on track.

What We Do

The Party Wall Act applies to construction projects that may impact shared or boundary structures, such as walls, fences, or adjoining land. Our services include everything from issuing formal Party Wall Notices to negotiating agreements and preparing legally binding Party Wall Awards. We cater to a wide range of projects, including:

  • Home Extensions and Loft Conversions
  • Garden and Boundary Wall Modifications
  • Chimney Breast Alterations or Removal
  • Foundation Excavations Near Property Lines

Whether you’re building a dream addition to your home or addressing concerns about a neighbor’s project, we offer tailored solutions that prioritize your rights and peace of mind.
