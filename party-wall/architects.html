Looking For A Party Wall Agreement For Your Customer?

----------------------------

Well, if you are an architect carrying out any work that will be done along a shared wall or boundary (for example, extensions, loft conversions or basements) in all likelihood yes, you would need to arrange for a Party Wall Agreement. That’s where we come in.

We are expert in assisting architects Lewes to make legal Party Wall etc. Act 1996 obligations less concerning. As your reliable local party wall surveyors, we help you work with us personally and with their client to make sure that it is all done right – from serving notice on adjoining owners through to preparation and agreement of Party Wall Awards.

We appreciate that party wall matters are delicate and can intrude on your life. We act as the magic fairy, streamlining the stages and avoiding neighbour disputes whilst timescales are not muddled with legal challenges
